How It Works

Three steps to a safer ride

🏍️
Step 1

Rider Registers

Riders sign up with their bike details and get a unique verified code and QR.

🔍
Step 2

Passenger Verifies

Before the ride, the passenger scans the QR or enters the rider code to verify identity.

📋
Step 3

Trip is Logged

A trip record with destination, fare, and timestamp is saved with a unique Trip ID.

Features

Everything you need to stay safe

✅

Identity Verification

Every rider is registered with real bike and personal details before getting a code.

🔐

QR Code per Rider

Each rider gets a unique QR code to show passengers for instant verification.

📍

Trip Logging

Every trip is saved with a unique ID, destination, fare, and exact timestamp.

🛡️

Accountability

Trip records can be used to trace incidents and support police investigations.
