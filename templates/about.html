About SafeRide

Why we built this and what we stand for

The Problem

In Bangladesh, informal roadside motorcycle rides are one of the fastest and most affordable ways to travel. But they come with a serious safety gap — no identity verification, no trip records, and no accountability if something goes wrong. Passengers don't know who they're riding with, and riders have no protection against false accusations.

Our Solution

SafeRide adds a digital safety layer to the existing informal system — without replacing it. Riders register with their real details and receive a unique verified code and QR. Passengers verify the rider before the trip and log a record with destination, fare, and timestamp. Every ride becomes traceable and accountable.
